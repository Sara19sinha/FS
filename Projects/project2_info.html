Introduction to Anne Hathaway

Anne Hathaway is a well-known American actress admired for her talent, versatility, and dedication to acting.
She was born on November 12, 1982, in Brooklyn, New York.
Coming from an artistic family, Anne was inspired by her mother, who was a stage actress.
From a young age, she showed interest in performing arts and actively participated in school plays and theatre.

Beginning of Her Acting Career

Anne Hathaway rose to fame with her debut film The Princess Diaries (2001), in which she played the role of 
Mia Thermopolis, a shy teenage girl who discovers she is a princess. The movie was a commercial success 
and made her popular among young audiences. Instead of sticking to similar roles, Anne chose challenging 
characters to grow as an actress.

Famous Movies and Awards

Over the years, Anne Hathaway appeared in many successful films such as The Devil Wears Prada (2006),
Becoming Jane (2007), The Dark Knight Rises (2012), Les Misérables (2012), and Interstellar (2014). Her role
as Fantine in Les Misérables earned her the Academy Award (Oscar) for Best Supporting Actress, proving
her excellence and commitment to her craft.
